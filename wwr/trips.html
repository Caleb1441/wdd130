Most popular trips!!

French Broad River — Ideal for families and beginners:(Gentle rapids and forested landscapes) This 3-hour trip offers calm stretches to enjoy the scenery and small rapids for excitement. We provide certified guides, included equipment, and stops along the route to rest and take photos.

Rogue River — Adventure for intermediate level:(Moderate rapids and technical sections) Approximately 4 hours long, with canyon views, adrenaline-filled rocky passages, and calmer stretches to catch your breath. Recommended for those with some experience or looking for a safe challenge.

Truckee River — Dynamic and accessible experience: (Ideal for mixed groups and young adventurers) A 2–3 hours route with fun rapids, controlled jumps, and alpine landscapes. Perfect to combine with local activities.
